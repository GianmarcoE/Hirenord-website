Why Hirenord

Our experience recruiting in the Nordics, Poland, Italy, makes us knowleadgeble and efficient at finding the right talent to fulfill your recruitment needs in a shorter time

Save time

Acting fast is a key requirement for today's organizations, and having a position filled-in quickly means optimizing both time and spendings

non-exclusivity

We believe there are multiple sources of skilled candidates, so we will never require you to give us an exclusive assignment. You will always be free to choose which and how many agencies and consultants can support you

Success fee

Only pay when you hire our candidate. If you find your talent autonomously, or through another agency or consultant, we will not charge you anything for our service

Networking

Our presence on professional networks and social media allows us to easily access a broad pool of skilled professionals

  • IT

    The high demand for IT professionals makes it challenging to find and hire the right candidates. We can support you with sourcing skilled developers, data engineers, analysts, cybersecurity specialists and more

  • Banking & Finance

    Our experience in the banking and financial industry allowed us to build a broad network of professionals working for the main financial institutions, banks and Big4

  • Renewable Energy

  • Language speakers

    We know the challenges related to hiring foreign language speakers and having them to relocate elsewhere, even abroad. We can assist you with sourcing candidates and promoting best practices to transform the relocation into an actual opportunity
